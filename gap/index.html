My Gap

A radio that doesn't look like a piece of "electronics" that fits the calm and minimal aesthetic of my bedroom

I haven't bought a radio for my bedroom since everything on the market is either too big or looks like electronics. I want to make something more subtle.

I have found 5 people who also have this gap: my boyfriend, housemate, colleagues at work and friends.

I have experimented with a low-powered computer that can play live radio streams. To address the gap I need to build a case for the hardware and some way of controlling it.

I'm very interested in the intersection between physical products that are connected and networked in some way with novel user interfaces.
